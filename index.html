    Band Ember

    Ember is a vibrant Tunisian band known for its captivating fusion of traditional Tunisian music with modern rock and pop influences. Comprising talented musicians, Ember creates an energetic and engaging atmosphere with their dynamic performances. Their music often explores themes of identity, love, and social issues, resonating deeply with audiences. With a unique sound that blends rich cultural heritage with contemporary styles, Ember is making a significant mark on the Tunisian music scene and beyond.

    DJ Zus

    DJ Zus is a prominent Tunisian DJ and music producer known for his eclectic style and ability to blend various genres, including electronic, hip-hop, and pop. He has gained popularity in the Tunisian and North African music scenes for his energetic performances and unique sound. DJ Zus is often recognized for his contributions to the local nightlife and music festivals, where he engages audiences with his vibrant mixes and infectious beats. His work has helped elevate the visibility of electronic music in Tunisia, making him a significant figure in the country's evolving music landscape.
